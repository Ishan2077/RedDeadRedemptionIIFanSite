America, 1899. The end of the Wild West era has begun. After a robbery goes badly wrong in the western town of Blackwater, Arthur Morgan and the Van der Linde gang are forced to flee. With federal agents and the best bounty hunters in the nation massing on their heels, the gang must rob, steal and fight their way across the rugged heartland of America in order to survive. As deepening internal divisions threaten to tear the gang apart, Arthur must make a choice between his own ideals and loyalty to the gang who raised him.

Review Scores

Upon release, Red Dead Redemption II recieved critical acclaim from critics and fans alike. It is the highest rated video game this generation, with Grand Theft Auto V, having a score of 97/100 on Metacritic and an average user rating of 8.4/10 .

Development Cycle

After the critical success of Red Dead Redemption, the team began working on what came out to be Red Dead Redemption 2. Lead writer and producer, Dan Houser, alongwith the team of several senior writers like Michael Unsworth, Lazlow Jones and Rupert Humphries. Initial story concepts and mission ideas were ready by 2011. The team then completely focussed on the critically acclaimed Grand Theft Auto V, which became the higgest grossing entertainment product in the history, grossing over $6 billion in revenue and more than 135 Million units sold. Red Dead Redemption II's development was lead by all Rockstar Studios combined.
